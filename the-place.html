ThePlace
A Room · Some Light · Plenty of Time
01 — The Light

South-facing windows. Old glass. Morning enters slowly.

The room is brightest between 9 and 11.

02 — The Threshold

A heavy teak door. A handle that has been touched a thousand times.

You'll feel the temperature change before you see the room.

03 — The Counter

Hand-thrown ceramics. Reclaimed oak. A scale, a kettle, a quiet rhythm.

Eight stools. Built by a friend in Pune.

04 — The Tables

Small enough for two. Big enough for a notebook and a flat white.

Communal seating in the back, by the plants.

05 — The Garden

Six steps down, a small green room. Olive, fig, a pot of tulsi.

Open until last light. Bring a book.
