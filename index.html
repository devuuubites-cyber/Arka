You walk in without thinking, and the noise outside falls away.

One door. A held breath. A different temperature.

A room built for slowness — wood, plant-light, soft chairs.

No queue energy. No hurry. Just somewhere to sit.

Beans ground this morning. Water at the right temperature. A barista who notices.

Single origin. House blend. Whatever you take it like.

That's the point.
